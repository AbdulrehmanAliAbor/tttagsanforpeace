// Our History

Foundations and Vision.

In our first year, we laid the groundwork by establishing our core values, mission, and objectives.

Our organization was born out of a shared vision to promote human rights and foster peace in communities affected by conflict and injustice.

// We've Accomplished

First Steps and Early Impact.

In our second year, we launched our initial programs aimed at providing immediate relief and support to vulnerable populations.

We focused on advocacy, legal assistance, and educational workshops to raise awareness about human rights issues.

// We've Accomplished

Advocacy & Policy Influence.

With a solid foundation and growing network, we intensified our advocacy efforts in our fourth year.

We engaged with policymakers, participated in international forums, and launched campaigns to influence policy changes that uphold human rights.

// We've Accomplished

Innovation and Global Reach.

Our global reach expanded through virtual partnerships and online campaigns, allowing us to connect with a broader audience and drive change on a larger scale.

We leveraged technology to enhance our programs, from digital platforms for education and advocacy to mobile applications for reporting human rights violations.

// We've Accomplished

Recognition and Awards.

Our dedication and impact have not gone unnoticed. We have been honored with several prestigious awards,

Recognized for our outstanding advocacy efforts and influence on policy changes that protect human rights.

Some Questions

What is the mission of TagsanForPeace?

Our Mission: Our mission is to promote and protect human rights and to foster peace across the globe. We strive to create a world where every individual can live with dignity, free from fear and oppression, through advocacy, education, and direct support.

How do we ensure transparency & accountability?

We're committed to operating with highest ethical standards. We ensure it by: Regular Audits, Annual Reports Donor Updates, & Open Communication.

Where does TagsanForPeace operate?

Our Reach: We operate both locally and internationally. Our programs are implemented in various countries, focusing on areas most affected by human rights violations and conflicts.

Can I partner with TagsanForPeace on a project or initiative?

We welcome partnerships with individuals, organizations, and businesses that share our vision. To discuss potential collaborations, please contact us at info@tagsanforpeace.org

How are donations used?

Donations are crucial to our work and are used to: Fund Programs: Direct support to our advocacy, education, and victim support programs. Operational Costs: Ensure the efficient operation of our organization. Emergency Aid: Provide immediate assistance in crisis situations. We maintain transparency in our financial reporting, and donors can see the impact of their contributions through our regular updates and reports.
